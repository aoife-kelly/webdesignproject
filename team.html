Meet The Team

Our Team

Endrew

Endrew is the co-owner of RaveCulture Galway, a leading force in rave culture, dedicated to bringing high-energy events to life. As the main DJ, Endrew sets the pulse of every event, curating electrifying sets that keep crowds moving until sunrise. With a passion for music and an eye for immersive experiences, they blend cutting-edge sounds with an unforgettable atmosphere, making every event a night to remember.

Joe

Joe is the co-owner and visual director of RaveCulture Galway, shaping the immersive visual experience that defines their events. From dazzling light shows to mind-bending stage designs, they craft the atmosphere that transports ravers into another dimension. With a passion for creativity and a deep understanding of rave culture, Joe ensures that every event is a sensory journey, blending music and visuals into an unforgettable experience.
